Introduction

We’re a group of six friends—Adithi, Dhanya, Eman, Sheetal, Elroy, and Thejas—spread across India, the UK, and Australia. Our mission? To create a dynamic fund that fuels epic group trips, adapting to income differences, global distances, and spontaneous plus-ones. This retro-inspired plan blends fairness, growth, and a whole lot of fun!

Fund Pooling Mechanism

  • Contributions: Each member invests any sum they wish, minimum 1% of income (less for Elroy and Sheetal in UK/Australia).
  • Tracking: No specific balances; funds tracked as units for trip budget allocation.
  • Growth: Funds grow with returns, boosting trip budgets yearly.
  • Transparency: Quarterly updates on fund appreciation shared by Thejas.

Contribution Calculator

Trip Planning and Budgeting

  • Planning: Any member can propose; participants opt in.
  • Budgeting: Costs incurred from units purchased by involved members (e.g., 4 members = cost split by their units).
  • Growth: Fund returns increase trip budgets annually.

Investment Strategy

  • Objective: Maximize returns for epic trips.
  • Management: Thejas oversees low-to-moderate risk investments (e.g., ETFs, bonds).
  • Updates: Fund balance appreciation reported quarterly.
  • Risk: Diversified portfolio, max 20% in high-risk assets.

Plus-One Policy

  • Eligibility: Any member can bring friends with early notice.
  • Cost: Pledge to double contribution henceforth (e.g., $2k becomes $4k with a plus-one).
  • Rules: Notify 1 month in advance; new funds allocated accordingly.

Subsidy Plan

  • Context: Adithi, Thejas, and Eman get flexibility.
  • Plan: No minimum investment for 1 year; others cover via higher contributions.
  • Post-1 year: All adhere to 1% minimum (or less for foreign dwellers).

The Epic Adventure Fund Agreement

  • Parties: Thejas, Adithi, Dhanya, Eman, Sheetal, Elroy.
  • Purpose: Pool funds for travel with flexible contributions.
  • Contributions: Min 1% income (less for UK/Australia), subsidies for 1 year.
  • Management: Thejas invests, reports quarterly.
  • Termination: 3-month notice or unanimous vote; units refunded proportionally.

Legally binding per terms.

Challenging Repercussions (Not Enforceable)

  • No-Show Without Reason: Dress as a retro mascot (e.g., disco llama) and lead the next trip’s karaoke night.
  • Last-Minute Cancel: Perform a 10-minute stand-up comedy routine about your excuse at the next meetup—or fund a group pizza night.
  • Fund Misuse: Run a “Fund Redemption Marathon” (5K in a ridiculous costume) and post it online for group approval.

For epic laughs and epic redemption!

Disclaimer

This Epic Adventure Fund (“Fund”) is a voluntary, informal agreement among friends and carries inherent risks and responsibilities:

  • Financial Risks: Investments may lose value due to market volatility, economic downturns, or poor management decisions. Returns are not guaranteed, and losses could reduce trip budgets significantly.
  • Participation: Trips are optional; members manage their own travel logistics, visas, and personal expenses beyond allocated units. Non-participation does not entitle refunds unless the Fund dissolves.
  • Plus-Ones: Members are fully liable for their guests’ costs, conduct, and compliance with group rules. Failure to increase contributions as pledged may result in exclusion from future trips.
  • Subsidies: Temporary for 1 year for Adithi, Thejas, and Eman, subject to group consensus; may be revoked if abused or if financial strain on others becomes unsustainable.
  • Legal: This agreement is binding to the extent of contributions and unit allocations, governed by applicable laws in members’ jurisdictions (India, UK, Australia). Disputes resolved by majority vote or mediation; no liability assumed by Thejas beyond reasonable care in fund management.
  • Fund Management: Thejas is not a professional financial advisor; investment choices are at group risk. Quarterly reports are for transparency, not legal accountability.
  • Dissolution: Fund dissolution requires unanimous consent; delays in refunding units may occur due to market conditions or liquidation timelines.
  • Miscellaneous: Members assume risks of travel mishaps, currency fluctuations, or unforeseen events (e.g., pandemics). Comedic repercussions are for entertainment and not enforceable; serious breaches may lead to exclusion by group vote.

Consult a legal or financial professional before committing significant funds. Participation implies acceptance of these terms and risks.
